// THE OVERHAUL

Vanilla survival,
rewritten as an RPG.

Classes & Kingdoms

Pledge to one of four kingdoms. Pick Knight, Ranger, Rogue, or stay a Peasant and suffer. Your class sets your health, damage, and speed — and a reskinned XP bar tracks your class level, not vanilla scraps.

Kingdom Gates

The Nether and the End are sealed. They open only when your kingdom's pooled class XP crosses the threshold. Progression is a server-wide war effort — nobody speedruns alone.

Voiced AI NPCs

Fourteen characters with LLM brains and ElevenLabs voices. Walk up, press talk, and speak — they answer out loud, hold grudges, remember favors, and hand out quests in real conversation.

Ironglow Dynamic Light

A built-in dynamic lighting engine. Torches burn in your fist, spells flare across cave walls, will-o'-wisps drag pools of light through the dark. No shader pack required.

Gear Quality & Forging

Every tool, blade, and ingot rolls a quality tier and wears down with condition. Master the blacksmithing minigame to forge masterwork steel — or swing a peasant's bent shovel forever.

Reactive Music

An adaptive score that listens to the world. Calm hamlet themes collapse into PvP climax stingers the instant blades cross. The soundtrack knows you're losing before you do.

Encumbrance & Weight

Items have weight. Exceed your class carry cap and you crawl home under Slowness like the greedy wretch you are. The HUD tracks every kilogram of loot-lust.

Seasons, Fishing & Bait

Living seasons, a fishing minigame with real loot tables, and 23 catchable butterfly species that double as Terraria-style bait. No bait, no bites.
