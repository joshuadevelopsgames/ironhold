# Prompts — Tax/toll icons

Four 16×16 icons at row A (y=0). Used as in-line section icons.

---

## ICON_LAND — `(48, 0, 16, 16)`

> Pixel art grass-block face, Minecraft style, 16×16. The classic
> Minecraft grass-on-dirt 3/4 view: top 5 rows = grass green
> (`#55DD55` base, `#88FF88` flecks), bottom 11 rows = dirt brown
> (use `#5C4A2B` body, `#3A2D14` flecks). 1px black outline.
> Looks like the front face of a grass block from the inventory but
> slightly more stylized — 3 grass tufts visible as 1-2 px specks
> on the top edge.

State: 1.

> **Palette note:** keep dirt browns to `#5C4A2B` / `#3A2D14` from
> the parchment range. They double as earth tones.

---

## ICON_CROWN — `(64, 0, 16, 16)`

> Pixel art royal crown, Minecraft GUI item style, 16×16, face-on
> view. Three-pointed gold crown with a horizontal band: 8 pixels
> wide at the base, 5 pixels tall. Three points rise from the band,
> each capped with a 1-pixel `#FFEE99` highlight (suggesting a gem).
> Band has a 1-pixel `#B8860B` shadow at the bottom and a 1-pixel
> `#FFEE99` highlight at the top. Body: `#FFCC44`. 1px black
> outline. Reads as regalia.

State: 1.

---

## ICON_GATE — `(80, 0, 16, 16)`

> Pixel art iron portcullis, Minecraft style, 16×16. A rectangular
> arched gate seen head-on: pointed-top archway 12 pixels wide. Three
> vertical iron bars (`#5C5C5C` body, `#3F3F3F` shadow on the right
> side of each bar) crossing two horizontal bars. Bottom of each
> vertical bar tapers to a 1-pixel iron spike. Body of arch: 1-pixel
> black outline + 1-pixel `#8E8E8E` inner highlight on the top arc.
> Reads instantly as "gate".

State: 1.

---

## ICON_PORTAL — `(96, 0, 16, 16)`

> Pixel art ender portal ring, Minecraft style, 16×16. A circular
> ring 12 pixels in diameter, drawn as 1-pixel `#4477CC` outer +
> 1-pixel `#7755CC` body + 1-pixel `#1F1F1F` inner void. The center
> 8×8 is filled with the dark void (`#1F1F1F` with 2-3 single
> `#FFEE99` star pixels scattered). 1px black outline on the
> outermost ring. Reads as a magical fast-travel portal.

State: 1. Used both as the section icon and per-portal-row marker.
